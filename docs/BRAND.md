# Libera — Brand Identity

## Brand Name
**Libera** *(LEE-beh-rah)* — from Latin *liberare*, "to set free."

6 letters. Pronounceable in every major language (no th/r-l ambiguity clusters, open vowels). Reads beautifully in App Store listings, domains (`libera.app`), and handles (`@libera`).

## Short Name
**Libera** (app icon label). System short name: `libera`.

## Tagline
> **Speak freely.**

Secondary taglines for campaigns:
- *Conversation, unchained.*
- *Every voice, everywhere.*

## Brand Philosophy
Communication is a human right, not a product feature. Libera is built on the belief that a private conversation is the natural state of conversation — and that privacy should feel effortless, warm, and beautiful, never technical or paranoid. Freedom here means three things: freedom **from** surveillance, freedom **to** connect across any platform or border, and freedom **within** the product — no dark patterns, no attention traps, no ads between friends.

## Mission Statement
To give every person on Earth a private, beautiful, and instant way to be heard — on any device, in any language, without compromise.

## Core Values
1. **Privacy by default** — end-to-end encryption is not a mode; it's the baseline.
2. **Craft** — every pixel, every animation, every millisecond is designed. Apple-grade fit and finish.
3. **Openness** — open protocols where possible, transparent security audits, portable data.
4. **Human first** — features serve connection, never engagement metrics.
5. **Universality** — one experience across iPhone, Android, iPad, macOS, Windows, and Web.

## Brand Story
Libera began with a simple observation: the world's conversations live inside products that treat them as inventory. We wanted a messenger that feels like a quiet, sunlit room — a place that belongs to the people talking, not to the walls. So we borrowed the discipline of Cupertino design, the speed of modern realtime infrastructure, and the mathematics of modern cryptography, and shaped them into something that disappears when you use it. The mark — an open loop with a single point of light — is the brand in one gesture: a conversation that is never closed, and a voice that is always free to leave, return, and be heard.

## Voice & Tone
- Calm, precise, warm. Short sentences. No exclamation marks in UI.
- Security explained in human terms ("Only you and Ava can read this"), never jargon-first.
- Never fear-based marketing.

## The Mark — "The Open Loop"
A near-complete ring, broken at the lower left, with a free-floating point at the opening.
- The **loop** = an infinite conversation, a chat bubble abstracted to its purest form.
- The **opening** = freedom; the conversation is never locked.
- The **point** = a single human voice — also the "typing" dot, the online dot, the notification dot. One geometric atom reused across the whole product.
- Rotated composition suggests a lowercase **e** / speech bubble with tail at small sizes.
